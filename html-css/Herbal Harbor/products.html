Herbal Teas

Green Tea

Our Green Tea is a delicate infusion of handpicked leaves, known for their fresh and grassy notes. Grown in the lush fields, it offers a soothing and revitalizing experience. Savor the pure, unadulterated essence of nature in every cup.

Black Tea

Experience the bold and rich flavor of our Black Tea. Hand-picked from select tea gardens, this classic blend offers a robust and full-bodied profile with a hint of malty sweetness. Whether enjoyed plain or with a splash of milk, it's a timeless brew that invigorates your senses and leaves you feeling refreshed and revitalized. Elevate your tea experience with our exquisite Black Tea.

White Tea

Indulge in the delicate and pure essence of our White Tea. Grown in high-altitude gardens, these tender leaves are handpicked and minimally processed to preserve their natural freshness. The result is a light and subtle brew with floral notes and a touch of sweetness. This gentle infusion offers a serene experience, making it the perfect choice for moments of tranquility and reflection. Embrace the grace of our White Tea and savor its ethereal taste.
